WEEK 0
DATA ACQUISITION
This is the starting point of your protocol.
Week 0 is where we collect your baseline data so CoachMax can build your protocol with precision.

This is not a max-effort week. Do not chase numbers. Do not test your ego.

Your job is simple: move clean, record honest data, and give CoachMax the information needed to build the right plan for your body, your schedule, and your real life.
No maxes. No failure. No guessing.

Keep 2 to 4 reps in reserve on all calibration lifts.

DIRECTIVES
Before your protocol is built, we need your starting point.

Complete each section below as accurately as possible. The goal is not perfection. The goal is honest data.
  • Starting photos
  • Body measurements
  • Calibration loads
  • Baseline cardio
  • Mobility and pain notes
  • Real training availability
Important:
If something hurts, do not force it.
If you do not know a movement, leave it blank.
If you are unsure about a load, choose lighter and keep control.

CoachMax needs clean data, not max effort.

Measurements

Calibration Loads
Choose a comfortable load. Keep 2 to 4 reps in reserve. I do not want your max. I want a clean load you can control.
If you do not know an exercise or you are not sure, ask Max. Do not attempt something that could get you injured.
Baseline Cardio
Choose one option. The goal is to create an easy starting point. You should finish under control. This is not a max test.
Mobility / Pain
If pain goes above 4/10 or feels sharp, stop the movement and write it down.
Schedule
